Singapore's Fiscal Position

Source: https://data.gov.sg/dataset/government-fiscal-position

What: Data
  • Represents the total revenue and expenditure spent across the years
How: Encode
  • Bar marks and encoded with a quantitative value attribute with one spatial position channel each
Why: Task
  • To find out the biggest amount of revenue and expenditure spent in one year

Year with Highest Revenue and Expenditure


What: Data
  • Represents the detailed spending of the revenue and expenditure from each department
How: Encode
  • Area marks and containment, with circular layout
Why:Task?
  • To provide a detailed side by side comparision between the revenue and the expenditure
Some tidbits
  • Zoom into circles by clicking on them. Clicking again on the same circle will zoom you out

  • Hover over a circle to see what it represents and how much money it's worth.

  • Greenish hues symbolize expenditure, while redish hues symbolize revenue.

  • This is based on the UK budget shown in milestone 1, instead done in a hierarchical visualization with the SG budget.

  • Hope that this visualization helps to provide you with some insight on SG's fiscal position for 2021!
