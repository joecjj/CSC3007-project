Singapore's 2021 Fiscal Position

Source: https://data.gov.sg/dataset/government-fiscal-position

What is this?
  • A storytelling visualization to showcase the Singapore government's revenue and expenditures for 2021.
Who is this for?
  • For a general audience, from novice users to domain experts, local or international.
How was it done?
  • Data from data.gov.sg for was cleaned then transformed into this hierarchical circle packing chart using d3.
Some tidbits
  • Zoom into circles by clicking on them. Clicking again on the same circle will zoom you out

  • Hover over a circle to see what it represents and how much money it's worth.

  • Greenish hues symbolize expenditure, while redish hues symbolize revenue.

  • This is based on the UK budget shown in milestone 1, instead done in a hierarchical visualization with the SG budget.

  • Hope that this visualization helps to provide you with some insight on SG's fiscal position for 2021!
